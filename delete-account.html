Delete Your Account - SolveIt Homework Help

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from SolveIt Homework Help (“the App”), please follow the steps below. This page explains how you can request account deletion and what happens to your data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the SolveIt Homework Help app.
  2. Go to the Menu (top-right corner) and select Contact Us.
  3. Send an email to solveithwhelp@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete My Account.
  4. Include your registered email address in the email.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • Your interaction history (questions asked, solutions provided)
  • Any uploaded files (e.g., PDFs, images)

What Data Will Be Retained?

For legal and operational purposes, the following data may be retained for up to 30 days after account deletion:

  • Logs of app usage (e.g., crash reports, diagnostics)

Additional Information

  • Local Data: If you have stored any data locally on your device (e.g., history files), you can delete it manually by clearing the app’s cache or uninstalling the app.
  • Third-Party Services: Data shared with third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Google AI) will be deleted in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about account deletion or the App’s data practices, please contact us at: solveithwhelp@gmail.com
